David at Poon Hill

I just spent a month in Nepal with Hamro Nepal and had a ball. I did Poon Hill trek, 2 days of jungle activities in Chitwan, 3 days of Kathmandu City sight seeings, and a 13 day EBC trek, and a full day Kathmandu Valley day trek, with all things from airport to airport organised by Keshav and his staff, They all went well, with professionalism and due care. I can not speak any more higher than the quality of the services provided.
